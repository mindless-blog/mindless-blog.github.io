Mindless

A weekly newsletter about mindfulness in the 21st Century

What is Mindless?

Mindless is a weekly newsletter featuring thoughts on mindfulness and implementing it in your daily life. Every Sunday, you'll receive a few paragraphs on a topic relating to mindfulness, meditation, or personal growth! We also like to include mindful media like music, video, and other art.

Who is behind this?

We're Ethan & Martin! We've been friends since the 3rd grade and have been passionate about mindfulness for some time now. We're excited about sharing our thoughts with the world!
