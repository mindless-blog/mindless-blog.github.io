What is mindless?

mindless is a weekly newsletter featuring thoughts on mindfulness and implementing it in your daily life. Every Sunday, you'll receive an email on a topic relating to mindfulness, self-awareness, or personal growth! We also like to include mindful media like visual art, videos, & music.

How does it work?

Our newsletter alternates between affirmations & articles. Affirmations are 1-minute reads that focus on quick bursts of positive energy. Articles focus on a topic and dive into conceptual knowledge & application. These usually take 5 minutes to read. All of our past articles can be found below! Subscribe to get our affirmations!

Who is behind this?

We're Ethan & Martin! We've been friends since the 3rd grade and have been passionate about mindfulness for some time now. We're excited about sharing our thoughts with the world!
